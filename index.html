CSCI Honors – Subway & Walkability Project

Hunter College · Fall 2025 · HC Assignments 3–7

Overview

This website presents my analysis of New York City neighborhoods along the 6 train line. I use walkability scores, Niche rankings, demographic data, and commute times to explore urban quality of life.

HC 3 – Computing Costs

In HC 3, I compared housing costs and commute times from several neighborhoods to Midtown (Empire State Building) and the Financial District (Federal Hall). The goal was to see how much extra rent New Yorkers are willing to pay for a shorter commute.

HC 4 – Subway Logo

For HC 4, I used matplotlib and hex color codes to design a subway line logo. The logo is based on the IRT Lexington Avenue (6) line.

HC 5 – Initial Website

This GitHub Pages site is the initial skeleton for my course projects. All code and analysis are version-controlled through GitHub.

HC 6 – Additional Data

I added demographic data (median income, median age, average rent, and population) for five neighborhoods along the 6 line: Financial District, Upper East Side, East Harlem, Parkchester, and Pelham Bay.
